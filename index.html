A video motion editor is responsible for editing and assembling recorded raw footage into a finished product that is suitable for broadcasting. I use my creative and technical skills along with complex editing software to organize stills, footage, dialogue, sound effects, graphics and animation effects.

Here are some of the common responsibilities of a video motion editor:

  • Design and animate 2D motion graphics for various media platforms and purposes.
  • Edit raw footage into a finished product that is suitable for broadcasting.
  • Create graphic templates and deliver motion graphics in different formats and specifications.
  • Prepare design plan, concept and layout for motion graphic project.
  • Research and brainstorm creative ideas, styles, and trends for motion graphic projects.
